## Program Description

This SMS program sends account-related notifications to users who have opted in. Messages include login verification codes, security alerts, and important account updates. No promotional or marketing messages are sent.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency is low and varies based on account activity. You will only receive a message when there is something that requires your attention, such as a sign-in attempt or a security change.

## Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

## How to Opt Out

Reply **STOP** to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent. To re-subscribe, reply **START** or update your notification preferences in your account settings.

## How to Get Help

Reply **HELP** to any message for assistance, or contact support through the website.

## Supported Carriers

Supported carriers include AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and other major US carriers. Carrier support may vary.

## Privacy

Your phone number will not be shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes. For full details on how your data is handled, see the [SMS Privacy Policy](/sms/privacy-policy).
