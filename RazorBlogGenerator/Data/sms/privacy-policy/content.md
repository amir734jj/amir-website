## Scope

This policy describes how phone numbers and SMS-related data are collected and used in connection with text message notifications sent through this service.

## Data Collected

When you opt in to receive SMS notifications, the following information is collected:

- Your mobile phone number
- Your opt-in date and method (form or keyword)
- Opt-out requests and the date they were received
- Delivery status of messages sent to your number

## How Your Data Is Used

Your phone number is used exclusively to send account-related SMS notifications. These include login verification codes, security alerts, and important updates about your account. No promotional or marketing messages are sent.

Your phone number and messaging history are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for any purpose, including advertising or marketing.

## Data Retention

Phone numbers and associated opt-in records are retained for as long as your account is active or as required to comply with legal obligations. Upon opt-out, your number is retained in a suppression list to ensure no further messages are sent, but is not used for any other purpose.

## Your Rights

You may opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. You may request access to or deletion of your SMS-related data by contacting support through the website.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of SMS notifications after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, use the contact form on this website.
